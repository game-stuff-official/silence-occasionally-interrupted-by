Silence Occasionally Broken By

A website made on a whim after watching a stream with one of those silly, "1 hour of silence occasionally broken up by x" videos playing in the background.

How To Use

Press Play Sound to start the player. The sound will repeat indefinetly until you press Stop Sound.

The number input is the interval between plays.

Check Random Playback to have the sound play in random intervals between 0 and the entered number.
